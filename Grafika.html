Translacija u homogenim koordinatama

\[\begin{bmatrix}x' & y' & 1\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}0 & 0 & d_{x}\\0 & 0& d_{y}\\ 0&0&1\end{bmatrix}\cdot \begin{bmatrix}x & y & 1\end{bmatrix}\tag{1}\]

\[P' = T(d_{x}, d_{y})\cdot P\tag{2}\]

\[T(d_{x}, d_{y})=\begin{bmatrix}0 & 0 & d_{x}\\0 & 0& d_{y}\\ 0&0&1\end{bmatrix}\tag{3}\]

\[P' = T(d_{x1}, d_{y1})\cdot P\tag{4}\] \[P'' = T(d_{x2}, d_{y2})\cdot P'\tag{5}\]

\[P' = T(d_{x1}, d_{y1})\cdot T(d_{x2}, d_{y2}) \cdot P\tag{6}\]

Skaliranje u homogenim koordinatama

\[\begin{bmatrix}x' & y' & 1\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}s_{x} & 0 & 0\\0 & s_{y}& 0 \\ 0&0&1\end{bmatrix}\cdot \begin{bmatrix}x & y & 1\end{bmatrix}\tag{7}\]

\[P' = S(s_{x1}, s_{y1})\cdot P\tag{8}\] ,gdje je \[S(s_{x1}, s_{y1}) = \begin{bmatrix}s_{x} & 0 & 0\\0 & s_{y}& 0 \\ 0&0&1\end{bmatrix}\tag{9}\]

\[P' = S(s_{x1}, s_{y1})\cdot P\tag{10}\] \[P'' = S(s_{x2}, s_{y2})\cdot P'\tag{11}\]

\[P' = S(s_{x1}, s_{y1})\cdot S(s_{x2}, s_{y2}) \cdot P\tag{12}\]

\[P'' = R(\theta_{2})\cdot R(\theta_{1}) \cdot P\tag{18}\]
